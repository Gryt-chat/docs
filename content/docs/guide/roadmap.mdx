This page tracks every major feature across Gryt. Use it to see what's already available, what's actively being worked on, and what's planned for the future.

| Emoji | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| ✅ | **Shipped** — available now |
| 🔨 | **In progress** — actively being worked on |
| 📋 | **Planned** — on the radar, not yet started |

Have a feature you'd like to see? Check the [feature requests](/docs/guide/feature-requests) page.

## Voice and audio

- ✅ HD voice (WebRTC, Opus, 48 kHz, configurable bitrate)
- ✅ Noise suppression (RNNoise AI)
- ✅ Echo cancellation
- ✅ Auto Gain Control
- ✅ Dynamic range compressor
- ✅ Noise gate
- ✅ eSports mode (low-latency audio visualization)
- ✅ Mute / deafen (server-synchronized)
- ✅ Voice activity detection and speaking indicators
- ✅ Voice state grace period (transient disconnects don't drop you from the channel)
- ✅ Device hot-swapping
- ✅ Loopback monitoring
- ✅ Configurable hotkeys (push-to-talk, disconnect)
- ✅ Audio visualization (spectrum, level meters)
- ✅ Push-to-talk (configurable hotkeys, browser + Electron)
- 📋 More hotkeys (mute, deafen, camera, screen share, etc.)
- ✅ Per-user volume control (0–200%)
- ✅ Audio cues for state changes (join, leave, connect, disconnect)
- ✅ Connect / disconnect sounds (configurable, custom sound files)
- ✅ Per-channel voice settings (required push-to-talk, bitrate presets, eSports mode, disable RNNoise)
- 📋 Soundboard (play audio clips into voice)

## Video and screen sharing

- ✅ Camera support with device selection
- ✅ Camera preview, mirroring, quality, FPS, and codec settings
- ✅ Screen sharing (video + audio)
- ✅ Screen share audio isolation (excludes Gryt voices on Windows/macOS desktop)
- ✅ Per-application audio capture (capture only a specific app's audio on Windows)
- ✅ Screen quality presets (native, 4K, 1440p, 1080p, 720p, 480p)
- ✅ FPS settings (30, 60, 90, 120 — experimental: 144, 165, 240)
- ✅ Focused video view and popout
- ✅ Popout video with volume control
- ✅ Drag-and-drop video grid
- ✅ Gaming mode (cursor hiding, motion-optimized encoding, higher bitrate)
- ✅ Hardware-accelerated codec selection (H.264, VP9, AV1)
- ✅ Custom max bitrate control
- ✅ Advanced screen share settings (codec, bitrate, degradation preference)
- ✅ SVC temporal scalability (L1T1/L1T2/L1T3 with per-receiver layer-aware forwarding)
- ✅ Native DXGI screen capture (bypasses Electron IPC, direct WebSocket transport)
- ✅ Hardware video encoding (H.264 / HEVC via NVENC, AMF, QuickSync — zero-CPU-copy GPU pipeline)
- ✅ Insertable streams (injects pre-encoded H.264 directly into WebRTC, skipping decode→re-encode)
- 📋 Virtual backgrounds (blur / image replacement)
- 📋 Go-live / stream to channel
- 📋 Video layout options (gallery, spotlight, sidebar)
- 📋 Clips (save and share screen-share moments)
- 📋 End-to-end encryption (SFrame / insertable streams)

- ✅ Docker Compose deployment

- ✅ Embedded server in desktop app (host from the client on Windows, macOS, and Linux — no separate install)
- ✅ Cloudflare Tunnel support
- ✅ S3-compatible storage (AWS S3, MinIO, Cloudflare R2)
- ✅ SQLite database
- ✅ Identity service (certificate issuance for client identity verification)
- ✅ Multi-network support (LAN + WAN)
- ✅ Health check endpoints
- ✅ Prometheus metrics + optional Grafana monitoring
- ✅ Isolated image processing worker (sharp runs in a separate service, crashes don't affect the server)
- 📋 Server-to-server proxying / federation
- 📋 Evaluate MinIO alternatives (Garage, SeaweedFS, OpenSTRG)
- 📋 One-click install scripts
- 📋 Admin dashboard (web UI for server management)
